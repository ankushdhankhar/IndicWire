**Prime Minister Modi and Sports Minister Mandaviya React to Vinesh Phogat Disqualified from Paris Olympics**

**Date: August 7, 2024**

**Location: Paris, France**

In a shocking turn of events at the Paris Olympics, Indian wrestler Vinesh Phogat faced disqualification from her much-anticipated 50 kg gold medal bout on Wednesday morning. Phogat, a favorite in her weight category, failed to meet the weight requirements, leading to a heartbreaking exit from the competition. The incident has drawn widespread attention and reactions from across the country, including statements from Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Union Sports Minister Mansukh Mandaviya.

### The Incident

Vinesh Phogat, who has been a beacon of hope for India in wrestling, was set to compete for the gold medal in the 50 kg category. However, during the mandatory weigh-in on the day of the bout, Phogat failed to make weight, leading to her immediate disqualification. This unexpected development has stunned fans and athletes alike, as Phogat had been in excellent form throughout the tournament.

### Prime Minister Narendra Modi's Reaction

Prime Minister Narendra Modi expressed his disappointment over the disqualification but extended his support to Phogat. In a tweet, he stated:

"Heartbreaking to hear about Vinesh Phogat's disqualification at the Paris Olympics. She has always made our nation proud with her dedication and achievements. We stand by her in this challenging moment and know she will come back stronger. India is proud of you, Vinesh."

### Sports Minister Mansukh Mandaviya's Statement

Union Sports Minister Mansukh Mandaviya also expressed his solidarity with Phogat. In an official statement, he said:

"It is indeed a tough day for Indian sports, but Vinesh Phogat has been a true champion and role model for many young athletes. Her journey is far from over, and we are confident that she will overcome this setback. The Ministry of Sports will extend all necessary support to Vinesh to help her bounce back and prepare for future competitions."

### Reactions from the Sports Community

The sports community in India and worldwide has shown immense support for Vinesh Phogat. Fellow athletes, coaches, and fans have taken to social media to express their empathy and encouragement for the wrestler.

Former Olympic medalist and wrestling coach Sushil Kumar said, "Vinesh has the heart of a champion. This setback is temporary, and I have no doubt that she will return stronger and more determined."

### Looking Ahead

Despite this setback, Vinesh Phogat's future remains bright. The support from her country and the international wrestling community is a testament to her talent and dedication. As she regroups and prepares for future challenges, Phogat's journey will continue to inspire countless aspiring athletes across India and beyond.

**Conclusion**

Vinesh Phogat's disqualification is a reminder of the challenges and uncertainties in the world of sports. While this moment may be disappointing, it also highlights the resilience and spirit of athletes who continue to strive for excellence. With the unwavering support of her nation, Phogat is expected to make a formidable comeback and continue her pursuit of Olympic glory.
